Welcome to

San Franasia

This is the place to go when you craves for exotic flavors

San Franasia is a web app for enthusiastic foodies to share their tasting experience and discover some must-go restaurants. To join us, simply register and start your cuisine crush here. As a registered user, you are granted for many features in this app. You may search and view most updated restaurant information. You can get accurate route details from your current location to the place of your choice on a map. Our rating and comment features welcome every user to view and edit. More features regarding connecting with friends and initializing tasting meetups are coming soon.
